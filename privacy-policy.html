Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Applies to RepLab for iOS and Apple Watch

The short version: RepLab stores everything on your device. No accounts, no servers, no third-party analytics, no ads. Your health and workout data never leaves your phone.

1. Overview

RepLab ("we," "our," or "the app") is a strength-training application developed by Bevsoft. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data RepLab accesses, why it accesses it, how it is stored, and what your rights are.

RepLab does not operate any backend infrastructure. No data collected or generated by the app is transmitted to Bevsoft or any third party.

2. Data We Access

RepLab accesses the following data on your device, solely to provide app functionality. All data remains on your device.

  • HealthKit — Heart Rate: Streamed live from your Apple Watch during active workouts. BPM readings are displayed in the app and saved as part of your session history in the Health app.
  • HealthKit — Active Energy Burned: An estimated calorie value is written to the Health app when you complete a workout. This credits your Apple Watch Move ring.
  • HealthKit — Workout Records: A "Traditional Strength Training" entry is saved to the Health app when you finish a workout. Cancelled workouts are never saved to HealthKit.
  • Workout Data (SwiftData): Your training plans, exercises, completed sets, weights, reps, and session history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

RepLab does not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or any other device data.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Workout data is stored exclusively on your device using SwiftData. No data is sent to any external server, cloud database, or third-party service by RepLab.

If you have iCloud enabled, Apple may sync your device's SwiftData store across your personal devices via iCloud Drive. This sync is entirely managed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Bevsoft has no access to your iCloud data.

4. HealthKit Data

RepLab requests permission to read and write specific HealthKit data types. We only access HealthKit data that is directly relevant to the workout you are actively performing or have completed.

HealthKit data accessed by RepLab is:

  • Never shared with any third party
  • Never transmitted off your device by RepLab
  • Never used for advertising, profiling, or analytics
  • Never used for any purpose beyond displaying your workout stats and writing workout records

You can review or revoke RepLab's HealthKit permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → RepLab.

5. No Account Required

RepLab does not require you to create an account, sign in, provide an email address, or share any identifying information. The app is fully functional without any registration or login.

6. Analytics, Advertising & Tracking

RepLab contains:

  • No third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar)
  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • No crash reporting services that transmit data off-device
  • No behavioral tracking, fingerprinting, or cross-app tracking of any kind

We do not participate in Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because RepLab does not track users.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, license, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with any third party for any purpose. We have no backend infrastructure and receive no data from your device.

8. Data Deletion

Because all data lives on your device, you are in full control of deletion:

  • Workout history and plans: Delete the RepLab app from your iPhone to remove all SwiftData records.
  • HealthKit records: Open the Apple Health app, find any workout or data entry attributed to RepLab, and delete it directly.

9. Children's Privacy

RepLab is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
